    Our Platform Capabilities

    Converse

    Converse is an intelligent Conversational AI, which can understand a customer’s questions, and deliver answers in any language, without explicitly selecting the language. Customers can respond via voice or text inputs to Converse in any language of their choice.

    Converse’s capabilities fits into contact center use cases including critical scenarios, reducing the need for live agents. Converse can work with businesses where the customer and questions inflows are high (high meaning more than 1000 questions a day).

    A-Eye

    A-Eye stands out with its object detection capability where it does not rely on document formats, but it looks for specific objects or entities within the document, while using NLP (Natural Language Processing)techniques to understand the written text, and detect the languages relying on Simple AI’s Converse engine.

    This eliminates the need to train the underlying OCR with document formats, and build 10x scalability for immediate consumption of A-Eye’s capabilities. Combining Simple AI’s platform capabilities, A-Eye can deliver 99.99% accuracy irrespective of industry or document differentiation
